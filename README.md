# 🎮 Video Game Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze global video game sales data and uncover insights into market performance across publishers, platforms, genres, and regions.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales trends, helping identify top-performing games, popular genres, leading publishers, and regional market preferences through interactive visualizations and KPI metrics.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze global video game sales performance.
* Identify top-selling games and publishers.
* Compare sales across gaming platforms.
* Understand genre popularity and market trends.
* Explore regional sales distribution and consumer preferences.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Data Modeling
* Interactive Visualizations

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Global Sales
* Total Video Games
* Total Publishers
* Total Platforms

### Visualizations

* Top Selling Video Games
* Sales by Publisher
* Sales by Platform
* Genre Performance Analysis
* Regional Sales Distribution
* Interactive Filters and Slicers

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Identified the highest-selling video games across all platforms.
* Analyzed publisher market share and performance.
* Compared platform-wise sales trends.
* Evaluated genre popularity across different markets.
* Explored regional contributions to global video game sales.

---

## 📈 Business Value

This dashboard demonstrates how Business Intelligence tools can transform raw sales data into actionable insights, enabling data-driven decision-making and market trend analysis within the gaming industry.
